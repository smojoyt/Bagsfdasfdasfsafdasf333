Privacy Policy (Effective July 1, 2025)

1. What We Collect & Why

When you shop or interact with Karry Kraze, we collect your name, contact details, shipping and billing addresses, payment info, and purchase history. If you sign up for our email or SMS list, we collect your preferences for updates and offers. We also gather device and browsing data (like your IP address and clicks) to improve site performance and customer experience. Everything we collect helps us fulfill orders, communicate with you, and make our shop better.

2. How We Use & Share It

We use your information to ship orders, process payments, send confirmations, and—if opted in—deliver promotional messages via SMS or email. Your data is never sold or rented. We only share it with trusted service partners (like payment providers and shipping carriers) to complete your purchase or provide services. You can opt out of marketing at any time.

3. Your Rights & Contact

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To make a request, just email us at contact@karrykraze.com. We keep order records as long as needed for business or legal reasons. This policy may be updated from time to time, and the latest version will always be available here.
