Frequently Asked Questions (Effective July 1, 2025)

Processing, Cancellations, and Refunds

How long will it take my order to ship?
Please allow 2-3 business days for your order to be processed and an additional 5-7 business days for delivery. Business days do not include weekends or holidays. We process and ship orders 7 days a week to get you your order as soon as possible.

If you need to alter your order information, email us at orders@karrykraze.com with your order number and inquiry.

No cancellations or refunds after order submission. All sales are final.

Shipping and Delivery

What is the shipping carrier you use?
Typically, all domestic and international orders are shipped by USPS. If USPS is not available in your region, we will ship via UPS.

All orders receive a tracking number which can be used to stay up to date with your delivery.

We cannot change the address or intercept packages once shipped. We are not responsible for stolen, lost, or damaged packages.

Available Payment Methods

We accept most major credit and debit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. You may also use Apple Pay and Google Pay. For additional payment questions, please email us at orders@karrykraze.com.
