FORME

FORME is an exploration of fluidity and structure, where design and material merge in perfect balance. The collection finds beauty in symmetry and curves, with each piece celebrating the natural simplicity of shape and space. Smooth, rounded gold lines embrace gemstones, tracing their silhouettes to create fluid, undulating forms that appear to shift with movement. Balancing minimalism with femininity, FORME blends sculptural design with effortless style.
